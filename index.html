About Us

Mr. Ajay Patel – Founder

Mr. Ravish Singh – Co-Founder

Mr. Kavish Singh – Director

Established in Surat, Gujarat, Kavish Fabrication is a trusted name in precision metalwork and structural solutions. We specialize in structural fabrication, dismantling of existing structures, sheet metal fittings, color coating services, and a wide range of high-quality fabrication work.

With a strong focus on customer satisfaction, we are committed to delivering projects on time while upholding the highest standards of safety and quality across all our operations.

Our Services

M/S Structure Fabrication

We build strong and reliable M/S structures for factories, warehouses, and commercial spaces—built to last and fit your exact needs.

M/S Structure Color

Our expert painting and coating services enhance the appearance and longevity of your structures with weather-resistant, high-performance finishes.

M/S Dismantle

Need to take down an old structure ? We dismantle steel structures safely and quickly, with minimal mess or disruption.

Roof & Side Sheet Fitting

Precision fitting of roofing and side sheets for all types of structures such as godowns and factories, offering protection, insulation, and a clean, aesthetic finish.

Anchor Fastner Installation

We install strong anchor fasteners that hold your structures firmly in place—safe, secure, and built to last.

Customized Steel Structures

Have a unique idea ? We design and build steel structures that match your exact requirements—no matter the shape or size.

Safety Installations

We add safety rails, ladders, walkways, and more to protect your workers and meet industry safety standards.
